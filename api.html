    API Documentation¶

    FOOOF Object¶

    FOOOF([peak_width_limits, max_n_peaks, …]) Model the physiological power spectrum as a combination of aperiodic and periodic components.

    FOOOFGroup Object¶

    FOOOFGroup(*args, **kwargs) Model the physiological power spectrum as a combination of aperiodic and periodic components.

    FOOOF Object Functions¶

    funcs.combine_fooofs(fooofs) Combine a group of FOOOF and/or FOOOFGroup objects into a single FOOOFGroup object.
    funcs.fit_fooof_group_3d(fg, freqs, …[, …]) Run FOOOFGroup across a 3D collection of power spectra.

    Analysis Functions¶

    analysis.get_band_peak(peak_params, band_def) Extracts peaks within a given band of interest, for a FOOOF model fit.
    analysis.get_band_peak_group(peak_params, …) Extracts peaks within a given band of interest, for a group of FOOOF model fits.
    analysis.get_highest_amp_peak(band_peaks) Searches for the highest amplitude peak.

    Synthesis Functions¶

    synth.params.Stepper(start, stop, step) Object for stepping across parameter values.
    synth.params.param_iter(params) Generates parameters to iterate over.
    synth.params.param_sampler(params[, probs]) Makes a generator to sample randomly from possible parameters.
    synth.gen.gen_freqs(freq_range, freq_res) Generate a frequency vector.
    synth.gen.gen_power_spectrum(freq_range, …) Generate a synthetic power spectrum.
    synth.gen.gen_group_power_spectra(n_spectra, …) Generate a group of synthetic power spectra.
    synth.transform.rotate_spectrum(freqs, …) Rotate a power spectrum about a frequency point, changing the power law exponent.

    Plotting Functions¶

    plts.spectra.plot_spectrum(freqs, power_spectrum) Plot a power spectrum.
    plts.spectra.plot_spectra(freqs, power_spectra) Plot multiple power spectra on the same plot.
    plts.spectra.plot_spectrum_shading(freqs, …) Plot a power spectrum with a shaded frequency region (or regions).
    plts.spectra.plot_spectra_shading(freqs, …) Plot a group of power spectra with a shaded frequency region (or regions).
    plts.fm.plot_fm(fm[, plt_log, save_fig, …]) Plot the power spectrum and model fit results from a FOOOF object.
    plts.fm.plot_peak_iter(fm) Plots a series of plots illustrating the peak search from a flattened spectrum.
    plts.fg.plot_fg(fg[, save_fig, file_name, …]) Plots a figure with subplots covering the group results from a FOOOFGroup object.

    Utility Functions¶

    utils.trim_spectrum(freqs, power_spectra, …) Extract frequency range of interest from power spectra.
    utils.get_settings(f_obj) Get a dictionary of current settings from a FOOOF or FOOOFGroup object.
    utils.get_data_info(f_obj) Get a dictionary of current data information from a FOOOF or FOOOFGroup object.
    utils.compare_settings(lst) Compare the settings between FOOOF and/or FOOOFGroup objects.
    utils.compare_data_info(lst) Compare the data information between FOOOF and/or FOOOFGroup objects.
