    FOOOF Object¶

    FOOOF([peak_width_limits, max_n_peaks, …]) Model the physiological power spectrum as a combination of aperiodic and periodic components.

    FOOOFGroup Object¶

    FOOOFGroup(*args, **kwargs) Model the physiological power spectrum as a combination of aperiodic and periodic components.

    FOOOF Object Functions¶

    combine_fooofs(fooofs) Combine a group of FOOOF and/or FOOOFGroup objects into a single FOOOFGroup object.
    fit_fooof_group_3d(fg, freqs, power_spectra) Run FOOOFGroup across a 3D collection of power spectra.

    Analysis Functions¶

    get_band_peak(peak_params, band_def[, ret_one]) Extracts peaks within a given band of interest, for a FOOOF model fit.
    get_band_peak_group(peak_params, band_def, …) Extracts peaks within a given band of interest, for a group of FOOOF model fits.
    get_highest_amp_peak(band_peaks) Searches for the highest amplitude peak.

    Synth Code¶

    Generating Power Spectra¶

    gen_freqs(freq_range, freq_res) Generate a frequency vector.
    gen_power_spectrum(freq_range, …[, nlv, …]) Generate a synthetic power spectrum.
    gen_group_power_spectra(n_spectra, …[, …]) Generate a group of synthetic power spectra.

    Parameter Management¶

    Stepper(start, stop, step) Object for stepping across parameter values.
    param_iter(params) Generates parameters to iterate over.
    param_sampler(params[, probs]) Makes a generator to sample randomly from possible parameters.

    Transforming Power Spectra¶

    rotate_spectrum(freqs, power_spectrum, …) Rotate a power spectrum about a frequency point, changing the power law exponent.

    Plotting Functions¶

    plot_spectrum(freqs, power_spectrum[, …]) Plot a power spectrum.
    plot_spectra(freqs, power_spectra[, …]) Plot multiple power spectra on the same plot.
    plot_spectrum_shading(freqs, power_spectrum, …) Plot a power spectrum with a shaded frequency region (or regions).
    plot_spectra_shading(freqs, power_spectra, …) Plot a group of power spectra with a shaded frequency region (or regions).

    Utility Functions¶

    trim_spectrum(freqs, power_spectra, f_range) Extract frequency range of interest from power spectra.
    get_settings(f_obj) Get a dictionary of current settings from a FOOOF or FOOOFGroup object.
    get_data_info(f_obj) Get a dictionary of current data information from a FOOOF or FOOOFGroup object.
    compare_settings(lst) Compare the settings between FOOOF and/or FOOOFGroup objects.
    compare_data_info(lst) Compare the data information between FOOOF and/or FOOOFGroup objects.
