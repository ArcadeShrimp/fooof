fooof.plts.templates.plot_spectrum¶

fooof.plts.templates.plot_spectrum(freqs, power_spectrum, log_freqs=False, log_powers=False, ax=None, **kwargs)¶

Plot a line plot of a power-spectrum.

Parameters:
  • freqs (1d array) – X-axis data, frequency values.
  • power_spectrum (1d array) – Y-axis data, power_spectrum power values.
  • log_freqs (boolean, optional) – Whether or not to take the log of the power axis before plotting. default: False
  • log_powers (boolean, optional) – Whether or not to take the log of the power axis before plotting. default: False
  • ax (matplotlib.Axes, optional) – Figure axes upon which to plot.
  • **kwargs – Keyword arguments to be passed to the plot call.
